Compare how the writers convey their different attitudes to the two schools

Posted: 2021-02-25   |   Tags: english, language, paper two, question four

In both sources, the surroundings are abysmal, yet the staff share very different attitudes to the school. In Source A, Sister Brendan is eager to show off her fantastic pupils to the inspector, whereas in Source B, the writer dreads school. The writer in Source A shows Sister Brendan’s zeal in the way she moves: she “glided through the school”.
